A corporate identity or corporate image is the manner in which a corporation,
firm or business enterprise presents itself to the public (such as customers and investors as well as employees).
The corporate identity is typically visualized by branding and with the use of trademarks,
[4]but it can also include things like product design, advertising, public relations etc.
